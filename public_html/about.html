About Us

Who are we?

We are Anec + Dote. We're more than just an online archive of literature. We take pride in curating our collection so that we are able to share our admiration of beautiful printed literature with you. Although print may be a dying art, our metaphorical doors are open for you to engage into a new perspective.

What do we do?

We provide an online literature shop to provide a new look into beautifully designed books for our customers. We can offer you the option to purchase these books individually as well as enrolling in our Archive subcription plan to ship books to your door weekly.

Our values

We're a small, humble bookstore that values appreciation towards print. Slowly, it feels like the public is starting to fall in love and admire print design. It's a very exciting time for us as more books are starting to be published with beautiful typography and illustrations.
